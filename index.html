🎓 The Academy — Simple Terms for Every Man

Forex Basics (The Exchange)

Imagine going to the airport and swapping your US Dollars for Euros. If the Euro becomes stronger while you are on your trip and you swap back, you make a profit. Forex trading is just doing that from your couch instantly.

What is Leverage (The Booster)

Think of leverage as a "booster seat." If you only have $10, leverage acts like a loan that lets you control a trade worth $1,000. It magnifies both your wins and your losses, so it must be handled with deep respect.

Prop Firms (The Backers)

Proprietary trading firms are companies that back talented traders. You pass an evaluation or trial, and they give you a funded account of up to $200,000 of their own money. You keep up to 80% of the profits without risking your own capital.

Stocks 101 (Owning a Piece)

When you buy a stock, you buy a tiny "slice" of a company like Apple or Tesla. When the company makes more money and grows, your slice becomes more valuable, allowing you to build stable, long-term wealth.
